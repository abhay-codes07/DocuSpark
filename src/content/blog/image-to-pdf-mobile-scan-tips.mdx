# Image to PDF best practices for mobile scans

Phone scans are convenient, but inconsistent lighting and perspective can hurt final output.

## Keep distance and angle steady

Hold the camera parallel to the page. Extreme angles create skewed text and uneven edges.

## Use neutral lighting

Natural daylight or diffused white light prevents yellow tint and shadows over text.

## Batch in one session

Capture all pages in one setting so brightness and sharpness remain consistent across the full PDF.

## Crop before conversion

Trim edges and background clutter before creating the PDF to keep files cleaner and smaller.
